Hallstatt

Riding through the Austrian Alps toward one of the most beautiful lakeside villages in Europe.

The Ride

A few weeks ago, I rode through the mountain roads of Austria toward Hallstatt. The weather kept changing every few hours — clouds, sunlight, cold wind, and sudden rain in the distance.

The roads were smooth, flowing through valleys, lakes, and mountain passes. Every corner opened into another incredible view. Some moments felt unreal, especially riding beside the lake with the Alps in the background.

Hallstatt itself felt calm and timeless. Small streets, quiet water, old wooden houses, and mountains surrounding everything. It was one of those rides where the destination mattered less than the feeling of the journey itself.
