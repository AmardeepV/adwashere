My Bikes

Green Cheetah

Kawasaki Ninja 650 (2025)

Quick off the line and sharp through every corner, Green Cheetah perfectly matches my riding style. I ride it across Europe, where the mountain passes and winding roads really bring this bike to life. It responds instantly, making every ride feel controlled yet thrilling. For me, it’s the perfect mix of speed, agility, and reliability—always ready for the next stretch of road.

THE SHERPA

Royal Enfield Himalayan 450

Sherpa is my companion for riding in India, built for both the chaos of city streets and the calm of the Himalayas. The moment I took it out of the hustle and into the mountains, it truly showed its character. Smooth, stable, and dependable, it handled the world’s highest terrain with ease. This is the bike I trust when the journey gets challenging and the roads turn unpredictable.
