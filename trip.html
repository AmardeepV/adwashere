Four Country Trip

Austria • Slovenia • Italy • Croatia
May 22 – May 26, 2026

This motorcycle adventure is a loop through some of the most scenic riding regions in Central Europe, combining alpine foothills, Mediterranean coastline, mountain curves, historic cities, and legendary Adriatic roads.

Starting in Graz, the route covers approximately 1,200 km across Austria, Slovenia, Italy, and Croatia, offering a mix of technical mountain sections, flowing countryside roads, and long coastal sweepers along the Adriatic Sea.

  • 1,200 km across four countries
  • Austrian mountain and countryside roads
  • Slovenian border-region riding
  • Italian Adriatic scenery around Trieste
  • Croatian coastal highway riding
  • Sea views, cliffs, islands, and mountain panoramas
  • Combination of alpine and Mediterranean landscapes
  • Three days of diverse and scenic motorcycle touring
